Our Story

A little about us

Every morning before the sun fully rises, we fire up the roaster. The beans arrive green, and by the time we unlock the front door, the whole block smells like fresh coffee. That's not a marketing line — that's just how we start our day.

Brewed & Co. grew out of a simple belief: coffee tastes best when it hasn't been sitting on a shelf for weeks. We roast in small batches, right here in the shop, so every cup you drink was beans just hours ago. It's a little more work, but you can taste the difference — and that's the whole point.
