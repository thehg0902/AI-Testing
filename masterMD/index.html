We Roast Before You Wake

Every morning at 5am, before the first customer walks in, our roaster fires up. We buy green beans from small farms we trust — places where people care about the soil as much as the cup — and roast them right here, in the back of the shop. By the time you order, your coffee was beans just hours ago.

Brewed & Co. started because we thought a neighbourhood deserved a place where the coffee was genuinely fresh, not just marketed that way. We're not chasing trends. We're just making the best cup we can, every single day, and hoping you can taste the difference.
